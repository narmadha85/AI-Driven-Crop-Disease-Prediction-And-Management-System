AI Driven Crop Disease Prediction and Management System
 PROJECT OVERVIEW

The AI Driven Crop Disease Prediction and Management System is a smart agriculture-based mini project developed using Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) technologies. The main purpose of this system is to identify crop diseases at an early stage by analyzing crop leaf images.

In this system, farmers can upload images of infected crop leaves. The AI model analyzes the image and predicts the type of disease affecting the crop. After prediction, the system also provides treatment suggestions, preventive measures, and pesticide recommendations.

This project helps farmers reduce crop damage, improve agricultural productivity, and make faster decisions. It also reduces the dependency on agricultural experts for disease identification.
PROBLEM STATEMENT

Farmers face many challenges in identifying crop diseases correctly and at the right time. Traditional methods of disease detection are time-consuming and require expert support, which may not always be available in rural areas.

Due to delayed disease detection:

Crops may get damaged
Agricultural production may decrease
Farmers may suffer financial losses
Diseases may spread to nearby plants
Excessive pesticides may be used

Therefore, there is a need for an intelligent and automated system that can quickly detect crop diseases and provide proper management solutions.
